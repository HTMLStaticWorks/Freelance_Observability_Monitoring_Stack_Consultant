Who We Are

Architects of Reliability

We are a specialized consultancy dedicated to transforming chaotic infrastructure into transparent, highly observable, and autonomous systems. We bring enterprise-grade SRE practices to growing tech companies.

Our Mission

To democratize enterprise-grade observability. We believe every engineering team deserves deep visibility into their stack, eliminating blind spots and empowering them to ship faster with confidence.

Our Vision

A future where infrastructure incidents are predicted and resolved automatically before users are affected. We strive to make "zero downtime" the standard, not the exception.

The Core Tenets

Our SRE Philosophy

We approach operations as a software engineering problem. Toil is our enemy, automation is our tool, and reliability is our product.

  • Error Budgets are Sacred

    We balance feature velocity with reliability using strict mathematical models.

  • Automate Everything

    If it needs to be done more than twice, we write a script or operator for it.

  • Symptoms over Causes

    Alerts should trigger based on user-facing symptoms, not underlying causes.
