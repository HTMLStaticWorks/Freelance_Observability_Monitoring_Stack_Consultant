Pricing FAQs

No, we do not charge based on the scale of your infrastructure. Our consulting fees are based purely on the complexity of the project and the time required for implementation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept wire transfers, major credit cards via Stripe, and crypto for enterprise clients.

Is there a long-term commitment?

Only for the Retainer and Enterprise plans (minimum 3 months). Audit and Implementation are one-off engagements.
