Pre-Consultation FAQ

What should I prepare for the discovery call?

Please have a high-level overview of your current architecture, average monthly cloud spend on telemetry, and specific pain points (e.g., missed alerts, slow dashboards).

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes, we standardly sign Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreements before discussing any specifics regarding your proprietary infrastructure.

How long does a typical implementation take?

Implementation timelines vary based on complexity, but a standard Prometheus/Grafana setup for a medium-scale K8s cluster typically takes 2-4 weeks from start to finish.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes, we offer monthly retainer plans that include continuous tuning, regular SLO reviews, and priority troubleshooting to ensure your observability stack remains optimized as you scale.
